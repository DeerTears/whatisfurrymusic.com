indieAnthro

indieAnthro (formerly Intro to Furry Music) is a curated playlist of furry musicians by trickyStoop, an indie tape label.

The playlist rotates its selection every few months, spotlighting new and under-appreciated songs by furry musicians making indie/DIY music.
