what is furry music?

Furry music is anything that wants to call itself "furry music".

Right now there is a booming scene of musicians who use their furry identity as the catalyst to creation.

By asking "what is furry music", I try to find the unique voice and sounds that these artists have.

I'm always looking for the most outstanding, unique, undoubtedly furry music.
