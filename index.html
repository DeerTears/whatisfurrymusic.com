What is Furry Music?

Furry Music is anything that wants to call itself "Furry Music".

Furry music is a booming scene of niche musicians, creating music of many styles and genres from all over the world.

The purpose of asking "What is furry music?" is to find unique perspectives, voices, sounds, and communities through music and furries.

What makes a musician "furry"?

"Furry music" is not an easy definition. Many people have different opinions on what constitutes "furry music". To me, it seems suggested by one of many attributes:

  • Visual use of anthropomorphic characters
  • Furry convention presence
  • Fursuit performances
  • Furry community involvement

Furry characters

Some musicians use an anthropomorphic character, or "fursona" as an online identity with their music.

To name only a few, this includes artists like:

  • Ashley Ninelives
  • Tenkitsune
  • kendall :3
  • russelbuck

Furry convention presence

Some bands perform at furry conventions and furry events as their first choice of venue. This includes artists like:

  • Rhythm Bastard
  • Trio Menagerie
  • Full Color
  • E Royale

Fursuit performances

Although not required, some artists wear a "fursuit" of their character on stage during their music performances. This includes artists like:

  • I've Made Too Much Pasta
  • Fly To The Night
  • Fons the Bun
  • Para Raccoon

Furry community involvement

Some musicians may not appear furry on the surface, but regularly engage with the community as a place to perform and find community. This includes artists like:

  • Jaylaflame
  • $uDO.NORMI
  • $leazy EZ (FENDIHOOSKIE)
  • Michael Cera Palin
  • Sarah and The Safeword
